Cheese Quesadilla Recipe

Quick and Easy Cheese Quesadillas

Cheese quesadillas using only two ingredients for a light meal. Serves 1-2.

Ingredients

  • 2 10-inch flour tortillas
  • 11/2 cup of shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Place one tortilla in a nonstick pan and set on stove with the burner off. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
  2. Turn heat to medium and place other tortilla on top. After about 5 minutes, gently lift edge of quesadilla to see if bottom tortilla is starting to brown. It should take a little longer, but keep a close eye on it.
  3. Once it is golden brown, flip the quesadilla and brown other side, checking frequently. Slide on to a cutting board. Let sit for a couple of minutes and then cut into quarters.
